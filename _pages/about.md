I am a PhD student at the [Technion](https://www.technion.ac.il/en/home-2/), lucky to be advised by [Yonatan Belinkov](https://belinkov.com/) and [Gabriel Stanovsky](https://gabrielstanovsky.github.io/) from the [Hebrew University in Jerusalem](https://en.huji.ac.il/).

My research focuses on analyzing language models to discover their flaws and study human-like biases. I'm particularly interested in decision-making and reasoning that may affect real-world applications. I have previously worked at [Meta AI Research](https://ai.meta.com/research/), where I analyzed attention patterns of translation models. I hold an M.Sc. from [Tel Aviv University](https://english.tau.ac.il/), where I worked with [Omer Levy](https://www.cs.tau.ac.il/~levyomer/) on probing the embeddings matrix of language models, discovering surprising spelling information within tokens.

I am always open to collaborations and enjoy discussing research, language models, and everything in between.

Feel free to reach out via email!
