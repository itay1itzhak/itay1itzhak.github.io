I am a PhD student at the Technion, advised by [Yonatan Belinkov](https://belinkov.com/) and [Gabriel Stanovsky](https://gabrielstanovsky.github.io/).

I'm interested in analyzing language models to uncover flaws and human-like behaviors, focusing on decision-making processes and real-world application challenges. Previously, I worked at Meta AI research on machine translation models and completed an M.Sc. At Tel Aviv University with [Omer Levy](https://www.cs.tau.ac.il/~levyomer/), exploring spelling information in matrix embeddings.
I'm always intersted in talking about research, languagde model and everything in between.
