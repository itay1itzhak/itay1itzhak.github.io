Itay Itzhak

I am a PhD student at the Technion, lucky to be advised by Yonatan Belinkov and Gabriel Stanovsky from the Hebrew University in Jerusalem.

My research focuses on analyzing language models to discover their flaws and study human-like biases. I’m particularly interested in decision-making and reasoning that may affect real-world applications. I have previously worked at Meta AI Research, where I analyzed attention patterns of translation models. I hold an M.Sc. from Tel Aviv University, where I worked with Omer Levy on probing the embeddings matrix of language models, discovering surprising spelling information within tokens.

I am always open to collaborations and enjoy discussing research, language models, and everything in between.

Feel free to reach out via email!
